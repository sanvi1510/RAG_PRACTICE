Butterflies and blossoms:
It is a fresh, whimsical spring-inspired theme that celebrates the beauty of flowers and delicate butterflies. It brings a bright, cheerful, and airy atmosphere, full of pastel colours, floral patterns, and floating butterfly accents. This theme captures the feeling of a blooming garden in spring, evoking lightness, joy, and natural beauty.


Fiesta Frenzy:
Mexican Fiesta is a bright, colorful, and lively theme inspired by Mexican culture. It brings together bold decorations, fun music, and a joyful party vibe. With sombreros, piñatas, marigold flowers, vibrant patterns, and upbeat tunes, the space feels cheerful and festive—perfect for celebrating with lots of energy and fun.

Shadow seekers:
This theme brings a mysterious, crime-solving vibe. The space feels dark, moody, and thrilling with clue boards, secret files, magnifying glasses, and hidden puzzles. It’s all about solving mysteries, following trails, and uncovering secrets—perfect for a fun, adventurous, and slightly dramatic atmosphere.


Global fusion:
The Global Fusion theme celebrates cultures from around the world in one vibrant, lively space. It mixes colours, patterns, music, and décor inspired by different countries—like lanterns, world maps, traditional motifs, and iconic landmarks. The vibe is joyful and diverse, showing how cultures blend together beautifully. It’s perfect for a colourful, welcoming event where everyone feels like they’re travelling the world in one place.
